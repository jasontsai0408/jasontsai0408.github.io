Brunei River Landscape

The photo was taken at February, 2020. Different from other country like Singapore or Thailand, Brunei has it's own style of daily life. What's more, we can see many traditional boats on the Brunei River, which is an icon of Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei Darussalam.

Date: 2020.02.19

Tze-Chiang Express at Sandiaoling Station

There are many beautiful sceneries in Taiwan, including our railway system. Sandiaoling is a station situated at Ruifang, New Taipei City. It's a train station that you can't drive to arrive at. The most fantastic thing about there is that there are magnificent landscape of Keelung River, and it is one of the popular station for the mountain climber.

Date: 2016.11.05

Old Train at Kaoshiung Harbor Station

Today's Kaoshiung Harbor Station is a big museum for those old trains or historic items. Also, we can learn about the history and the past of those railway culture, especially the region of Kaoshiung.

Date: 2020.01.30

Singapore, a multiculture world

First trip in Singapore really shocked me at that time, I saw people from India, China, Malaysia, and so on, and the city is very wonderful. In spite of the fact that I only had three days to enjoy this country, I would never forget everything I saw at that time.

Tamsui, A Lovely Place

Famous for its sunset and old street, there are always many people visit Tamsui in order to have some fun at here. However, we can watch this place on the other side.
